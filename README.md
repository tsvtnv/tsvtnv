### About

- 🛠  Self-employed at 17 — running **TsvWeb**, a subscription web design studio.
- 📦  Shipped **20+ production websites** for clients across the UK, Europe, and the US — from motorsport personal brands to AI-powered learning platforms to medical aesthetics e-commerce.
- 🎓  Year 12 at Streetly Academy Sixth Form — Maths, Physics, Computer Science.
- 📍  Birmingham, West Midlands, UK.

### What I build

End-to-end production sites in **Next.js + TypeScript + Tailwind + Drizzle + Postgres**, deployed on **Coolify** behind **Cloudflare**, with **Stripe** subscriptions, **Resend** for transactional email, and full **GSAP**-driven motion design. Every site is built around conversion: fast, accessible (WCAG 2.1 AA), SEO-clean, and shipped in days, not months.
